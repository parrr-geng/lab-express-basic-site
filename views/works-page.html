Works

Miyazaki co-founded Studio Ghibli in 1985. He directed numerous films with Ghibli, including Castle in the Sky(1986), My Neighbor Totoro(1988), Kiki's Delivery Service(1989), and Porco Rosso(1992).

The films were met with critical and commercial success in Japan.
